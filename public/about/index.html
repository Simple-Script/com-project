About Angwardai

Angwardai is a simple artificial intelligence built for a school project.

How it Works

Angwardai can maintain its small size because it reads data from a webserver. It has been trained from over 100,000 articles from Wikipedia.

Why Should You Use It?

It may not be the best AI but its not connected to an official company, not selling data, and has a simple ui with no ads.
